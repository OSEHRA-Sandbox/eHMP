Click the Patient Care Area to View Additional Patient-Care Information

Note:

The Patient Bar plug-in is a work in progress. Specific features may undergo minor or significant changes as the plug-in iterates toward national release. The following descriptions and instructions apply solely to the software's state as it existed at the time of this writing.

When you click the Patient Bar's patient-care section, the application displays a pop-up pane containing the following information:

  • Primary care team (from the Patient Care Management Module (PCMM))
    • If the patient has no assigned primary care team, the application displays the words: No Primary Care Team Assigned
  • Primary care team phone number (if available— (nnn) nnn-nnnn format)
  • Primary care provider
    • If the patient has an assigned primary care team but does not have an assigned primary care provider, the application shall display the words: No Primary Care Provider Assigned
    • If the patient has a primary care provider, the application displays the provider's pager and office phone numbers
  • Associate provider
    • If the patient has an assigned primary care team but no assigned associate provider, the application displays the following words: No Associate Provider Assigned
    • If the patient has an associate provider, the application displays the associate provider's pager and office phone numbers
  • Attending physician
    • If the patient has an assigned attending physician, the application displays the attending physician's pager and office phone numbers
  • Inpatient provider
      If the patient has an assigned inpatient provider, the application displays the provider's pager and phone numbers
  • Mental health treatment team
  • Mental healt treatment coordinator
    • If the patient has an assigned mental health treatment coordinator, the application displays the coordinator's pager and phone numbers

Clinical Practice Environment

With its CPE (Clinical Practice Environment) application, the HMP Team Facing framework delivers software that serves healthcare teams and helps meet their needs in caring for our veterans. CPE software will help transition Veterans Health Administration from a chart-centric model of care to a team-based, patient-centered model of care through advanced clinical software and a modern software architecture. This software represents an effort to reestablish close clinician-IT collaborative relationships and to build field-driven, web-based modules that meet the following functional and technology goals.

  • Functional Goals:
    • Comprehensive, coded, integrated data
    • Knowledge-driven, context-based decision support
    • Role- and conditions-based workflow-driven systems
    • Multi-patient, team-based care
  • Technology Goals:
    • Open source, with a service-oriented architecture (SOA)
    • Web-based, with a flexible, scalable architecture
    • Context-aware, with an extensible, modular platform
